Leadership

IBBN is stewarded by servant-leaders committed to integrity, unity, and national transformation. Below is the founding vision-bearer; the organisation chart lists approved role titles. Named officeholders will be published here when cleared for public release.

Founding vision-bearer

Prophet Dr. Isa El-Buba Sadiq

Conceived through his eminence Prophet Dr. Isa El-Buba Sadiq from a visionary burden for national rebirth, IBBN exists to restore lost values, revive founding ideals, and inspire a new generation of leaders for a better Nigeria.
